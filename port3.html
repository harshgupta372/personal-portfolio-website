About this TextUtils App

Project information

  • Scripted through: JavaScript
  • Inspired By: Word Manipulator
  • Framework Used: React
  • Project URL: https://textutilsbyharshgupta.netlify.app/

This interactive React project cleans and transforms your text ! Remove extra spaces, adjust capitalization (uppercase/lowercase/proper nouns), or reverse the order of words. Feeling fancy? Make the computer speak your text aloud! This versatile tool also lets you copy the modified text to your clipboard for easy pasting. Tame unruly text and have fun experimenting with different transformations !

Designed by Harsh Gupta
